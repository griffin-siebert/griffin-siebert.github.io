Griffin Siebert

Artist Content Creator Filmmaker & Designer

Born: Salt Lake City, UT 1993

Self taught painter, content creator, filmmaker, and mixed media artist based out of Salt Lake City, UT. Born and raised in Utah, Griffin has a deep connection to the surrounding mountains and wilderness.

Learning to paint as a child, and being brought up by the local snowboard scene in Utah, Art and design has always been a cornerstone in Griffins life. Griffins paintings and works have been cultivated by board sport culture and the nature surrounding the Utah area. Snowboarding has taken Griffin around the globe on an endless search for good snow and new faces. Creating independent content for various brands that Griffin is affiliated with has forced him to learn skillsets and tools that help him achieve his creative goals.

Current Athlete & Content Creator for Quiksilver, & Nitro Snowboards
